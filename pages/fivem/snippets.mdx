## taskBar
```lua
exports['vc-taskbar']:taskBar(length, name, runCheck?, keepWeapon?, vehicle?, vehCheck?)
```
- length: `number`
- name: `string`
- runCheck?: `boolean`
- vehicle?: `boolean`
- vehicle?: `boolean`
- vehCheck?: `boolean`

Example:
```lua
local finished = exports['vc-taskbar']:taskBar(5000, 'Eating...')
if finished then
    -- do something
end
```
